**Pedestrian Stairwell**
Make pedestrian networks seamless and stylish with this compact 1x1 Pedestrian Stairwell designed for connecting underground pathways.
**Features**
- Found under Landscaping → Pathways for easy access.
- Built with vanilla assets for a refined, cohesive look.
- Accurate pedestrian path ensures realistic stair usage; now with 2x capacity.
- Well-lit stairwell for safe, nighttime visibility.
- Small footprint allows flexible landscaping, props, and surface design nearby.
- Essential for busy intersections—place at corners of major roads to eliminate crosswalks and keep traffic flowing smoothly.
- Enhances accessibility—connect additional entrances for a subway station.
- Six building-color variations.
- Fully tested and operational. This is not just a visual asset. Citizens walk up and down the stairs if connected properly. Best used with divided roads with the crosswalks removed [as shown].
- Localized in 12 languages for a global audience.

**Usage**
- Remove crosswalks: use the advanced road services and select the crosswalk icon; right-click near the -crosswalk.
- Place the stairwell on an intersection corner or along a street. A tunnel stub is included. Connect it to -other underground pavement tunnels at –6.25 m depth [or select -18.75 feet].
- The entrance is accessible from the front at ground level and through the rear underground tunnel stub from the left and/or right.
- Contains a park component that enables pedestrian passage through the asset for functional realism. No park maintenance is required.

This stairwell repurposes the vanilla subway entrance mesh into a clean, versatile pedestrian access point—perfect for integrating an underground pathway network with minimal space impact.
